Notations

A series of interactive applications that study how artists use graphical notations to structure their animated films. An alternative approach to a film script that leverages on visual structures for the purpose of storytelling.

Mr. Bug Goes to Town

One of the earliest examples of notations for an animated film. Created by the Fleischer studio for their feature length film Mr. Bug Goes to Town (1941).

A Chairy Tale

Notations by Norman McLaren for the film A Chairy Tale.
